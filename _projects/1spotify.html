Spotify Karaoke Feature

Jan 2021

The Challenge

Our goal was to design a new feature on an already existing global app, so we chose Spotify. It was intended for our featureto be intricate enough, while still sticking to the existing brand design system.

The Solution

The new Karaoke feature will allow users to read the lyrics and sing their favorite songs solo or in group, via desktop or mobile.

Duration

2 months

Role

Feature Ideation | Information Architecture | Personas | Desktop UI | Wireframes | Prototyping | Usability Tests
