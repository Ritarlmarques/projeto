Flowr / Online Bouquet Shop

Jan 2021

The Challenge

The project consisted in selecting a physical product, creating and designing a brand with an online shop that sells it. Both mobile and desktop versions were developed using only the elements created for a Design System.

The Solution

FLOWR is an online flower bouquet shop with the mission of making the purchase of flowers as simple and personalized as possible while keeping sustainable and ecological values.

The user has the freedom to make the main decisions in the creation of his flower bouquet. In the end, he only has to wait between one to five days for his branch to arrive by bike.

Duration

1 month

Role

Concept | Design System | Sketches | Wireframes | Mockups
